### "Significance" (triage definition)
Treat an item as *significant* if it includes at least one of:
- Human implant / first-in-human report, or substantial clinical follow-up
- FDA IDE/clearance milestone, pivotal trial initiation, or major regulatory change
- Clear durability/longevity/materials result that affects chronic recording/stimulation stability
- A funding or partnership event that plausibly changes execution (major round, strategic partnership, acquisition)
